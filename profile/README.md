# [Antiwork](https://antiwork.com)

## Make work play.

Antiwork started with [Gumroad](https://github.com/antiwork/gumroad). In 2025, we open-sourced everything - our entire suite of tools that helps run and scale Gumroad.

## 🌈 Contribution Guidelines

We love our community and welcome contributions!

Please watch our [live streaming PR review sessions](https://www.youtube.com/@anti-work) in order to contribute.

We reward [bounties](https://antiwork.com/bounties) based on contributions.

To ensure people only get paid for bug-free code, bounties may take up to 90 days to get paid. To request a bounty, email bounties@antiwork.com with your pull request link and the related issue details.

Join our [Telegram group](https://t.me/+EhFVHSsck2IzYWZh) for announcements and discussions.
